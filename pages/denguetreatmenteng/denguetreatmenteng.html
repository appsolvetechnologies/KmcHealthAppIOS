DENGUE

HOW IS DENGUE TREATED?

  1. Rest
  2. Plenty of fluid to drink e.g.Water, Glucose,ORS, Fruit juice, Dab water.
  3. Ensure urination of the patient every 1-3 hours.
  4. Dehydration is the primary cause of complications or death due to dengue and not low Platelet count. Usually, Platelet count may remain normal during first three days of fever. Platelet count drops after three days of fever.If no dehydration, Platelet will start rising from the sixth day.
  5. Patient must take at least two days rest when free of fever.
