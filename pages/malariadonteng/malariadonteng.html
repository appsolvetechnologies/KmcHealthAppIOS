MALARIA

DON’TS FOR MALARIA PREVENTION

  • Avoid water stagnation to prevent breeding of mosquitoes.
  • Don’t let water stagnate.
  • Avoid contaminated blood transfusion.
  • Don’t keep unscreened doors and windows open at night.
  • During pregnancy, don’t travel to any malarious region.
  • Do not wear clothes that expose arms and legs.
