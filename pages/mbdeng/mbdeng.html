WHAT YOU NEED TO DO

FOR PREVENTION AND CONTROL OF MOSQUITO BORNE DISEASES (MBD)

  • Visit a KMC-run Ward Health Unit soon after you catch sudden fever and avail free facilities for blood test for malaria/dengue and platelet count.
  • Remember, early diagnosis and complete treatment (EDCT) of malaria and treatment of falciparum malaria with ACT (Artemisinin Combined Therapy) are mandatory.
  • If you contract vivax malaria, take Primaquine tablets for 14 consecutive days.
  • For detection of dengue NS1 antigen and dengue IgM antibody, get your blood tests done by ELISA, not by any Rapid Diagnostic Test Kit.
  • In case symptoms like sudden onset of high fever, severe headache, joint pain, rash, bleeding from nose, gum or with stool develop, consult a physician immediately.
  • If you catch dengue/malaria, please send the report to the KMC-run health centre located in your ward.
  • Use mosquito repellents liberally to prevent mosquito-bites at daytime.
  • Empty and clean water storage containers properly at a weekly interval.
